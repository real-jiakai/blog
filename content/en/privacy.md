This site records visits automatically for audience measurement, reliability, and content improvement. There is no analytics consent prompt. Depending on the service, the recorded data can include the page and referring page, time of visit, interactions or session playback, browser and device details, an IP-derived approximate location, and technical diagnostic data.

The production site uses:

- [Microsoft Clarity](https://privacy.microsoft.com/privacystatement) for interaction analytics and session playback.
- [Google Analytics](https://policies.google.com/privacy) for traffic and audience measurement.
- A self-hosted [Umami](https://umami.is/privacy) instance for traffic measurement.
- [Cloudflare Web Analytics](https://www.cloudflare.com/privacypolicy/) supplied by the delivery infrastructure.

Reactions and comments are handled separately by emaction and GitHub Utterances when those features appear on an article. Following an external link subjects the visit to that destination's privacy practices.

Browser privacy controls, content blockers, or network policy can prevent some requests. Questions about this site's data practices can be sent through the contact methods on the [About page](/en/about/).
