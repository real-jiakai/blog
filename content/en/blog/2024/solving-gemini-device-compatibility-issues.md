## Background

Google launched the Gemini Advanced plan on February 8, 2024, providing access to Google's most powerful AI model Ultra 1.0. The Gemini Android App was also launched simultaneously.

On their phone, downloaded Google Assistant App and Google App sequentially, entered Google Assistant App for initialization settings, but didn't switch language as mentioned in the post [entered Google App settings in top right corner to switch language, but found the language column was blank].

## Personal Experience

Yesterday on Chinese New Year's Day (February 10th), after updating the Gemini App on my Pixel 7 phone, I found that as long as I switched the Google Assistant language to English (United States) on my Pixel 7, the app could run normally on my phone.

![Switch Google Assistant language to English (United States)](https://cdn.sa.net/2024/02/11/q65Yda2ItsUk4fi.webp)

![Gemini App running normally on my Pixel 7 phone](https://cdn.sa.net/2024/02/11/KPDSk6auI7WnxC5.webp)

My domestic Vivo phone can't be flashed to upgrade the Android system, so the Android version is still 10, which no longer meets the minimum system requirement for Gemini's second Android App version [Android 12]. Maybe as Gemini Android App iterates, it will support lower Android versions.

After sharing a typical Gemini Advanced vs GPT-4 case I saw online, you'll get an initial understanding of why I say Gemini Advanced is inferior to GPT-4.
